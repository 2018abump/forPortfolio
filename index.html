Meet the Artist

Alyssa Bump, also known by his online-persona MorePastel or as Lys or Elijah Shilling, is an American digital artist. Since a young age he's been applying fantastical ideas to paper, inspired by the plethora of shows and games available to him. Through various medias, he harnessed a competitive drive to always improve and artistically express. However, he recgonized in late highschool that what motivates him most are his friends whom he considers found family; reflecting that they had strong relationships that all started because of their shared interests, Alyssa realized they can help others do the same. Alyssa strives to provide art that not only he enjoys, but to be a part of something bigger than himself that would allow others like him, the high-school out castes or those with nerdy fixations, to help find their own found family. That's when Alyssa found the passion behind the creation of video games.

During 2018-2022, Alyssa obtained a BSc of Fine Arts and Video Game Design. He's helped to officially publish four games, including a board game, during his time in college and has made assets for many other works. When not focusing on providing concept art for projects or acting as art lead, Alyssa spends much of his time as a digital freelance artist and gifting his friends with illustrations. He adores the concept of watching what he makes come to life, and more importantly that it causes people to smile or engage. As an LGBT transgender individual, he recognizes the importance of making safe spaces and providing a way for people to reach out to each other. With strong team work skills and a passion to create, he strives to make these goals of his a reality.

During his spare time, Alyssa can often be seen drawing, gaming, writing, or investigating the mysteries of the human past. He's a bit of a pantomath and enjoys learning a variety of subjects such as virology, psychology, and most of all, cultural anthropology. He's a loving and dear father to quite a few plants, and the amount of children keeps growing.

If you have any questions, concerns, or would like to get in contact with me for commissions, then feel free to reach out to me on my social media and email!
