Meet the Artist

Alyssa is an American-Canadian digital artist that specializes in fantasy character design and illustration. Since a young age she's been applying fantastical ideas to paper and inspiring others with her work. Through various medias, she harnesses a competitive drive that allows her the freedom to express creatively and impactfully. Alyssa strives to provide art that not only she enjoys, but to be a part of something bigger than herself that would allow others like her to help find their inspirations.

During 2018-2022, Alyssa obtained a BSc of Fine Arts and Video Game Design. As art lead of several projects, she has worked with others to officially publish both video and board games, and has provided aid in creating assets for others. Outside of group projects, Alyssa spends much of her time as a digital freelance artist. She adores the concept of watching what she makes come to life, especially if it leaves a lasting impact. Life is full of inspiration and magic, and Alyssa's goal is to help others see it.

During her spare time, Alyssa can often be seen drawing, gaming, writing, or investigating the mysteries of the human past. Alyssa is a quick learner and enjoys teaching herself new things.

If you have any questions, concerns, or would like to get in contact with me for commissions, then feel free to reach out to me on my social media and email!
